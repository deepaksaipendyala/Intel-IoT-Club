Who Are We

Intel Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

The goal of this club is to identify and support students who are passionate about working with developer communities to foster learning, sharing, and collaboration. We believe that innovation is happening at the forefront of academia, and in collaboration with Intel, student ambassadors can transform the future of technology. The IoT track provides students a great opportunity to enhance their deep-learning skills and learn about the cutting-edge hardware and software products by Intel that are driving the development of innovative IoT and AI solutions to solve real-world problems.
